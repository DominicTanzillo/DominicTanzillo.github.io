2025 Fortin Foundation Bass Connections Showcase Winner
Awarding Body Duke University Bass Connections
Date April 2025

Awarded best showcase work for VR-HEAL (Virtual Reality Health Education for Advanced Learning), a project developing immersive VR simulations for training on tunneled catheter procedures. Recognized for innovation in medical education that reduces dependence on expensive cadaver labs and live patient observation.
