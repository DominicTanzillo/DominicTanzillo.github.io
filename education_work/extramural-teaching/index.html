Extramural Teaching Activities

Duke Coursera: Introduction to Space Medicine
Type Online course development and instruction
Platform Coursera (Duke University)
Dates Released Fall 2021
Learners Open enrollment, global audience
  • Co-created the world’s first undergraduate-taught Coursera course with collaborator Nick Saba.
  • Six-week course covering the history of space medicine, aerospace physiology, astrobiology, and the health hazards of interplanetary travel.
  • Full certificate kept free for all students, an unprecedented decision in Duke’s Coursera catalog.
  • Produced with support from Duke Learning Innovation.
  • Featured in Duke SOM Magnify, CBS News, and the Duke Chronicle.

TRISH Space Health 101 Online Lecture Series
Type Online lecture series, external partnership
Partner Baylor College of Medicine, TRISH
Dates 2020–2021
Learners Open enrollment via TRISH Orbit Platform
  • Partnered with Baylor’s TRISH to convert the Duke Space Health 101 House Course into a publicly available online lecture series.
  • Collaborated with physicians, astronauts, and astrobiology researchers as guest lecturers from institutions worldwide.
  • Course materials available on the TRISH Orbit Platform.

Spherical Cows Podcast
Type Educational media production
Platform Spherical Cows Podcast
Dates 2021–Present
  • Co-host of a companion podcast to the Space Medicine Coursera course.
  • Features guest speakers in bioastronautics and addresses questions from enrolled students.
  • Extends course content beyond the classroom to a broader public audience.
